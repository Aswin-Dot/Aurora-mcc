Aurora

— Aurora —

Aurora, named after the Greek goddess of dawn is an Inter & intra collegiate cultural event organized by Madras Christian College solely for women students since 2010. It is Chennai's biggest cultural event organized exclusively for women. For the past five years, the event has been successfully bringing out the unique talents of women students.

Aurora includes a line up of events which includes cultural, literary and debating and informal events. We set up a stage that fulfills every dancer's dream. The bright lights, flashy clothes and the runway is what everyone waits for. We turn down the volume so that the mime artist can tell their story. The musical performances are so divine that it transcends all our senses. We have myriad of literary and debating events such as creative writing, poetry writing and oration. And informal events such photography, meme creation, face painting and collage making.

The ultimate aim of Aurora is to bring out the inborn talents of girls by giving a platform to these talents. The overwhelming participation for Aurora each year is a testimonial for its existence.

Suthanthira

Being an independent woman is freeing off from the clutches of society and stereotypes. There is nothing more rare or more beautiful than a woman being unapologetically herself, comfortable in her perfect imperfection. She is clothed with strength and dignity, and she laughs without fear for the future. She is not afraid of walking the wild side and no frightened by any posing monstrous threat. Because she knows she can handle it off with ease. She stands up for herself and for others around her as well. She voices out, unbothered about being labelled a trouble maker. She is a warrior with a brave front and at the same time she emotes, not failing to express her personal front. An independent woman is everywhere, in our homes, in society, in workplaces, and in us. Yes, there’s one in each among us and let’s cherish the treasure.

CULTURALS:

Dance

Dance has aesthetic and symbolic value, and is acknowledged by performers and observers within a particular culture.

Music

Music is a form of entertainment that puts sounds together in a way that people like, find interesting or dance to.

Mono-Act

The monoact is a scene with only one person physically present who is acting and reacting as though there was another person there.

Fashion-Icon

Being a fashion icon means setting trends and staying stylish for years to come. This is a place to showcase your fashion knowledge.

LITERARY AND DEBATING:

Creative-Writing

Creative writing is any writing that goes outside the bounds of normal professional, journalistic, academic, or technical forms of literature.

Oration

An oration is a speech delivered in a formal and dignified manner.

Poetry-Writing

Poetry is a form of literature that uses aesthetic and often rhythmic qualities of language.

INFORMALS:

Painting

Painting, the expression of ideas and emotions, with the creation of certain aesthetic qualities, in a two-dimensional visual language. Bring out the artist inside you.

Collage-Making

A collage is a visual representation made from an assembly of different forms, materials and sources creating a new whole.

Meme-Creation

Bring out the meme creator inside you and amaze us.

Photography

Photographers record events and tells stories using images. Tell us your story.

About Us

One of the oldest and most reputed colleges in India, Madras Christian College carries with it a rich legacy that continues to shine bright even to this day. The college has been the guiding light to many young minds, moulding them to serve the society with an undaunted spirit and strong moral values. For nearly 182 years, MCC has ensured the holistic development of several million students, embracing both traditions and modern approaches with the right balance.
