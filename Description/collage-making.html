collage-making

EVENT DESCRIPTION:

  • Individual event.
  • One entry per person.
  • Theme – SUTHANTHIRA (an independent girl).
  • Use of only A4 size chart paper is allowed.
  • Newspapers, magazines, decorative materials can be used.
  • Usage of other decorative/drawing materials.
  • Photo Limit is 1. In case of multiple photos, only the first pic will be considered.
  • Judging Criteria: Theme Incorporation, Composition, Naturality, Overall Impact.

SUBMISSION:

  • The last date for submission is 27-01-2021 (12PM).
  • The photo must only be submitted in jpeg format.
  • Rename the photo as "(Your name – college name) – AURORA 2021"
  • Mail the photo to aurora@mcc.edu.in
  • The subject of the mail should be "AURORA 2021 – FACE PAINTING".

GENERAL RULES:

  • The participant must be a women student from any college.
  • Photoshop manipulation and adding additional elements is not allowed. However, basic editing, enhancement, and cropping is allowed without affecting the genuineness of the photo.
  • Entries must be the original work of the entrant and must not infringe third-party's rights.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.

BONUS:

  • A chance to get featured on our Media pages.
  • In addition to sending the photo on mail, participants can choose to upload the same entry from their PUBLIC Instagram accounts. Top entries shall be published from the official Aurora page on Instagram.
  • Participants need to tag @aurora_mcc_ in the photo.
  • The hashtags #auroa2020, #cus20-21, #mcc need to be used in the caption.
