Dance

EVENT DESCRIPTION:

  • Individual event.
  • Any style of dance is accepted.
  • The theme is SUTHANTHIRA (an independent girl).
  • Judging Criteria: Choreography, Energy, Expressions, Clarity, Completion of Steps, song related to theme and overall impact.
  • Time limit is 4 – 5 minutes from the moment music starts.

SUBMISSION:

  • The last date for submission is 27-01-2021 (12PM).
  • Rename the recorded video as "(Your name – college name) – AURORA 2021".
  • Mail the video to aurora@mcc.edu.in
  • The subject of the mail should be "AURORA 2021 - DANCE".

GENERAL RULES:

  • The participant must be a women student from any college.
  • Only 1 entry is allowed per participant.
  • Vulgarity and obscenity at any point are strictly not allowed. It can lead to immediate disqualification.
  • The video must be recorded from a static camera. Videos having multiple angles/moving cameras are strictly not allowed.
  • Note that the video should not be edited in any way. Use of any kind of special effects is STRICTLY prohibited and will lead to direct disqualification.
  • The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.

BONUS:

  • A chance to get featured on our Media pages.
  • In addition to sending the video on mail, participants can choose to upload the same entry from their PUBLIC Instagram accounts. Top entries shall be published from the official Aurora page on Instagram and official CUS YouTube channel.
  • Participants need to tag @aurora_mcc_ in the video.
  • The hashtags #auroa2020, #cus20-21, #mcc need to be used in the caption.
